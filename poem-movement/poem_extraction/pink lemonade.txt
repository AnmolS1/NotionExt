pink lemonade

mujhe kabhi nahin pata
ki tum mere jeevan
ko kitna gadabad karoge
yah doston ke roop mein shuroo hua
lekin phir jaise hee main aapke liye
girane laga
aap uskeee liye gir gaye
isse zyaada main kya keh sakta hoon?
aap mere sabase achche dost
ban gaye,
aur main chaahata hoon
ki aap khush rahen.
lekin, priy pink lemonade,
mujhe tumse pyaar karta hoon.
yah theek hai,
main iske baare mein
kuch nahin karoonga.
main aik baar phir se peedit hooonga,
main use tumse pyaar
karane doonga.
lekin yah jaan lo,
main tumhen hamesha
pyaar karoonga.
yah kabhi nahin badalega.
