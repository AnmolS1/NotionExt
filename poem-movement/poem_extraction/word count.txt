word count

the questions jumble together
i stare at these for hours
every day attempting
to remember who i am
the names begin to blur
forming new ones
georgia hopkins
rice institute of technology
uc stanford
ut vanderbilt
all the while keeping track
no more than 250 words
between 100 and 300 words
600 word limit
i no longer know who this is
this person i've created
it seems as though he could
be a real person somewhere
but i know the truth
he's just better at being me
