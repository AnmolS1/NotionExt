a hopeful lunatic (nothing)

some call him smart
others call him a friend
others call him interesting
others still call him curious
but none call him
what he truly is
he would describe himself
as a hopeful lunatic
because while he knows what to say
he manages to say the worst thing
in the worst way possible
which is really quite odd
because even he knows
he has quite a way with words
he is not stupid for his education
was not to be frowned upon
but still
he manages to fuck up
despite what he thinks of himself
and what he hopes he might be
everyone knows
what he truly is
